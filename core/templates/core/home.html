How It Works

Our Smart Course Selector chatbot uses advanced AI language models to understand each student's academic progress and personal preferences. It analyzes major/minor requirements, remaining credits, and availability to offer tailored course combinations each semester.

Seamless Integration

The system connects with existing university course databases and degree audit systems. Students can interact with the chatbot through a simple interface that feels more like a conversation than a checklist. As students ask questions or request recommendations, the AI responds with intelligent, real-time feedback.

Why It Matters

Planning a class schedule can be overwhelming. With limited seats, prerequisite chains, and strict graduation timelines, many students feel lost. This tool provides structure and confidence. It’s more than a scheduler — it’s a digital advisor that understands your academic journey.

Built for the Future

Smart Course Selector isn't just a tool — it's a foundation for future academic support. With ongoing updates, we aim to include predictive analytics, internship matching, and workload balancing features to ensure student success beyond just class registration.
