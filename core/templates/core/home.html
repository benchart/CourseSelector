How It Works

Our Smart Course Selector chatbot is powered by Ollama 3.2, an advanced AI language model by Meta that can interpret academic progress, degree paths, and student interests. Our implementation of it allows for analyzing of remaining credits, course prerequisites, and availability to recommend the most efficient and personalized semester schedules.

Seamless Integration

The system integrates directly with university course databases and degree audit tools. Students interact with the chatbot through a natural, conversational interface. Whether they’re asking for recommendations or checking graduation requirements, Ollama 3.2 responds with intelligent, real-time guidance.

Why It Matters

Planning a class schedule can be overwhelming. With limited seats, prerequisite chains, and strict graduation timelines, many students feel lost. This tool provides structure and confidence. It’s more than a scheduler — it’s a digital advisor that understands your academic journey.

Built for the Future

Smart Course Selector isn’t just a tool — it’s a foundation for future academic support. With ongoing updates, we aim to include predictive analytics, internship matching, and workload balancing features to ensure student success beyond just class registration.
